Acquisition Trends

Weekly Acquisition Trend by Platform

üí° Trend Analysis

Week 41 (Oct 6-12) was the strongest acquisition week with 4,431 new users. There's a clear declining trend starting in Week 42, dropping to 2,377 users in Week 43, and 1,719 in the partial Week 44. iOS consistently outpaces Android by roughly 2.7:1 ratio across all weeks.

üí° Paid Ads Campaign Context

Week 41's strength is directly attributable to paid advertising support. On October 7th, the majority of paid campaigns were turned off, leaving only the best-performing campaigns on TikTok and Meta. On October 8th, Meta, TikTok, and Apple Search Ads were completely shut down. Finally, on October 15th, the remaining Google Ads campaign was turned off. The sharp decline in organic user acquisition following Week 41 demonstrates how paid advertising efforts create a halo effect that drives organic growth‚Äîwhen paid campaigns stopped, organic acquisition declined by 46-47% week-over-week.

Key Recommendations

üéØ Strong Activation Performance

With 26.41% activation rate, organic users are engaging well with the platform. Continue optimizing the onboarding experience and consider A/B testing different activation flows to push this even higher.

üí∞ Leverage Texas Market Dominance

üì± Optimize iOS Experience

‚ö° Address Declining Trend

The 32.7% week-over-week drop (Week 42 to 43) signals concern. The data clearly shows paid advertising's halo effect on organic growth. Consider strategic paid campaigns or seasonal promotions to revitalize organic acquisition.

üöÄ Activate High Engagement Users for Referrals

With 61.44% highly engaged users, they're prime candidates for referrals. Launch a referral program targeting this segment to drive organic growth through word-of-mouth.

üéÅ Expand Feature Adoption
